About Patrick


Patrick Star is SpongeBob's best friend as well as one of his neighbors. He is a naive pink sea story. He lives under a brown rock in the underwater city of Bikini Bottom. SpongeBob and Squidward are his neighbors. Both Patrick and SpongeBob bother Squidward, though they do not like to do it and they do it unknowingly and have good intentions


Patrick's portrayal is pleasant, overweight, lazy, unmannerly, naive, and generally ignorant. He lives under a rock on 120 Conch Street. Unlike most of the other main characters of the series. Patrick does not have a nose. Patrick has trouble with even the most rudimentary tasks and displays little common sense or intelligence. His memory can be appalling and he can forget how to do the most instinctual of actions, such as eating or how to fall over. He once forgot that he ate a chocolate bar the second after he ate it in.
